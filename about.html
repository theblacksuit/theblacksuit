About - The Black Suit

The Black Suit.

Powerful.

Sophisticated.

Sleek.

This is how I hope my channel will make you feel after watching my videos.

I'm GeGe--a lawyer working in California. I created this channel to help professional women, particularly lawyers, figure out how to navigate workplace situations and career-related matters. In my years of practicing in the legal industry, I've often encountered tricky issues but lacked accessible or reliable sources for sincere and practical guidance. So I started this channel to help others in my position. When I'm in court, I always wear a black suit, with a black top and shoes. It makes me feel empowered and sets me up for success. My goal on this channel is to share my experiences, anecdotes, and advice to empower you and set you up for success. Welcome!
